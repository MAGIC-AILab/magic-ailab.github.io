Welcome to MAGIC Lab

Research on robot manipulation, embodied learning, and general-purpose control.

MAGIC Lab studies how intelligent systems can perceive, reason, and act in complex physical environments. We are interested in manipulation, multimodal learning, decision making, and scalable training pipelines for capable embodied agents. Our goal is to build generalist robotic models that can understand the world, reason in human-centric ways, and carry out meaningful real-world tasks.

Research

We build methods for robust manipulation and general intelligence control across simulation and real-world systems, with an emphasis on bringing AI into the physical world through robotics.

  • Robot manipulation with vision-language-action and action reasoning models.
  • Embodied AI systems that perceive, reason, and act in human environments.
  • Failure diagnosis, recovery, and interactive improvement for robot policies.
  • Generalist control and scalable evaluation for real-world deployment.
